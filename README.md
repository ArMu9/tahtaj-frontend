# Tahtaj Platform

Tahtaj is a modern platform connecting users with trusted products and services.  
Designed to grow into a scalable commercial ecosystem, our platform emphasizes transparency, reliability, and brand readiness.

## Features
- Reliable product and service listings
- Scalable architecture for future expansion
- User-friendly interface
- Brand-ready structure for legal and commercial use

## Pages
- `index.html` – Home page
- `products.html` – Products and offerings
- `about.html` – About Tahtaj
- `landing.html` – Landing page

## Styles
- `styles/base.css` – Base styling and layout
- `styles/theme.css` – Colors, branding, and theme
