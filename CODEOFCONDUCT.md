# Code of Conduct

Tahtaj Platform is committed to fostering a safe, inclusive, and respectful environment for all users and contributors.

## Expected Behavior
- Be respectful and considerate
- Use welcoming language
- Respect different viewpoints and experiences
- Report unacceptable behavior

## Unacceptable Behavior
- Harassment or abusive language
- Discrimination based on race, gender, religion, or any personal characteristic
- Disruptive or harmful actions toward others

## Reporting
- Report violations to: conduct@tahtaj.com
- All reports will be reviewed and addressed confidentially

## Enforcement
- Violators may be warned, suspended, or banned depending on severity
